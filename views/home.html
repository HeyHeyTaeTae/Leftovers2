Leftovers

Take the gross out of groceries

We all buy groceries but remembering to eat them before they go bad can be a tricky task.
Leftovers keeps track of your groceries and sends you reminders near the expiration date so you don't have to.
Simply enter your groceries and the expiration date and enjoy keeping one more thing off your plate.
Reduce waste, fill your tummy, have money leftover.
